Lawn Mowing

Lawn mowing is the process of cutting grass to a desired height using a lawn mower. It helps maintain the health, appearance, and growth of a lawn by preventing overgrowth, encouraging thicker grass, and reducing the risk of weeds, pests, and diseases.

Edging

Lawn edging is the process of creating clean, defined boundaries between the grass and other areas like flower beds, walkways, or driveways. This is typically done using an edging tool or a string trimmer to trim the grass along these edges.

Hedge Trimming

Hedge trimming is the process of cutting and shaping hedges, shrubs, or bushes to maintain their desired shape and size. This is done using tools like hedge shears, electric trimmers, or manual pruning shears.

Debris Removal

Debris removal involves clearing away unwanted materials like leaves, branches, grass clippings, or other yard waste from a property. In lawn care, it typically refers to cleaning up after lawn maintenance tasks like mowing, trimming, or after storms that may leave scattered plant debris.
